  • Ongoing Project, And other Journey
    • ML Sound Program for senior
    Canopus

    It's sound recording ML program for patient on the bed.

    Before patients move foward another journey, it helps them to reflect their lives. As patients stays lying on the bed, they have still good ears.
    This project suggest to keep one's life in a good memory. So they could go the new steps.
    As the project title, Canopus is a star symbolizes luck and good journey in astrology.

    tech: ML, Spatial Sounding system, MAX for mix
    Process: 1. On the Personal life basis, sound sources would be recorded in field. The site-specific approach is related locality and place where a patient has lived. 2. The music sources are crafted by MAX 8. 3. The generated music is set and delivered to listeners; patients and family. 4. The play-set would be a souvenir for listeners and historical materials for history research related the site. * F.Y.I This time, the site is specified in center district named Yaksu-dong, Jung-gu in Seoul Metropolitan. ref. Schacher, J. C., & Kocher, P. (2006). Ambisonics spatialization tools for max/msp. Omni, 500(1). https://www.youtube.com/watch?app=desktop&v=MrZlcBlRrMk
